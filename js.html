Doodle_Suero

Welcome everyone, this is my final project for CSC106. This is a doodle game where you press start and you get put into a mostly empty screen where you can draw. At the top you will have your time, your score and your random word to draw. To draw you just click on your mouse and drag it which will make any shape you want, after 20 seconds a new word will appear and after 100 seconds the game is done.
